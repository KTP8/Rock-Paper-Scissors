The Rules!




Player Choice


Players select one of 5 'hand signals' to represent either rock, paper, scissors, lizard or spock:

  • Rock: A Closed Fist (Purple)
  • Paper: A Flat Palm (Light Blue)
  • Scissors: A Fist with Index and Middle Fingers Extended (Orange)
  • Lizard: A Hand with Fingers Flat and Thumb Pinched to Index Finger (Green)
  • Spock: A Flat Palm with Space Between the Ring and Middle Finger & Space Between Index Finger and Thumb (Dark Blue)



Winning


The Rules for the Game are as follows (see the scenarios in which each selection wins below):

  • Scissors cuts Paper
  • Paper covers Rock
  • Rock crushes Lizard
  • Lizard poisons Spock
  • Spock smashes Scissors
  • Scissors decapitates Lizard
  • Lizard eats Paper
  • Paper disproves Spock
  • Spock vaporises Rock
  • Rock crushes Scissors
